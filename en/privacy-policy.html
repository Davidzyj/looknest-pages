LookNest Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 26, 2026

Overview

LookNest is a local-only wardrobe and outfit planning app. It does not require an account and does not collect personal data.

Data Stored On Device

Closet items, outfit logs, language selection, and style preferences are stored locally on your iPhone. This data is not uploaded by LookNest.

Network And Tracking

LookNest does not include ads, analytics, tracking, third-party SDKs, or proactive network requests. Privacy and support pages open only when you tap their links.
